Our Services & Treatments

We offer a comprehensive range of services to treat a variety of conditions. Our goal is to create a personalized treatment plan that is right for you.

Conditions We Treat

  • Back & Neck Pain (Sciatica, Disc Issues)
  • Sports Injuries (Sprains, Strains, Tears)
  • Post-Operative Rehabilitation (Knee/Hip Replacement, ACL)
  • Arthritis & Joint Pain
  • Headaches & Migraines
  • Workplace Injuries (RSI)
  • And many more...

Our Treatment Techniques

Manual Therapy

Hands-on techniques including joint mobilization and soft tissue massage to reduce pain and restore movement.

Exercise Prescription

Customized exercise programs designed to strengthen, improve flexibility, and correct movement patterns, forming the core of your recovery.

Dry Needling / Acupuncture

A technique used to release tight muscles, reduce pain, and improve nerve function by inserting fine needles into trigger points.

Shockwave Therapy

A modern, non-invasive treatment effective for chronic tendon issues like plantar fasciitis and tennis elbow.
