About Kheng PhysioCare

Our Philosophy

At Kheng PhysioCare, our mission is simple: to provide the highest standard of patient-centered physiotherapy care in Cambodia. We believe in a hands-on, evidence-based approach that not only treats your symptoms but also addresses the root cause of your condition. We empower you with knowledge and tools to manage your health long-term, ensuring a lasting recovery and preventing future injuries.

Our Team of Experts

Our greatest asset is our team. Each therapist is highly qualified, passionate, and committed to continuous learning to bring you the most effective treatments available.

Dr. Kheng Bora, PT, DPT

Founder & Lead Physiotherapist

Dr. Bora founded Kheng PhysioCare with a vision to bring world-class physiotherapy to Phnom Penh. With a Doctorate in Physical Therapy from a leading university in Australia, he specializes in complex spinal conditions, manual therapy, and sports performance optimization. He is passionate about mentoring new therapists and advancing the field of physiotherapy in Cambodia.

Leakhena Som, PT

Senior Physiotherapist

Leakhena is an expert in helping patients recover fully after surgery. Her meticulous and caring approach ensures patients feel safe and confident throughout their rehabilitation journey. She is also certified in both Dry Needling and Pelvic Floor Physiotherapy, offering specialized care for a wide range of conditions.
