Welcome to Kheng PhysioCare

We're excited to partner with you on your journey to better health. Being prepared for your first appointment helps us make the most of our time together. Here's what you need to know.

Your First Visit: What to Expect

Your initial appointment will last approximately 45-60 minutes and will include:

  1. Comprehensive Assessment: We'll begin with a detailed discussion about your medical history, your current condition, and your personal goals.
  2. Physical Examination: Your physiotherapist will conduct a thorough physical assessment to evaluate your movement, strength, and areas of pain or restriction. This helps us get to the root cause of the problem.
  3. Diagnosis and Treatment Plan: Based on the assessment, we will explain our diagnosis in clear, understandable terms. We will then collaborate with you to create a personalized treatment plan.
  4. Initial Treatment: Your first session will almost always include some form of initial treatment to start providing relief and kick-start your recovery process.

What to Bring

Please bring the following to your first appointment:

  • Any doctor's referral letter (if you have one).
  • Results of any relevant medical imaging (X-rays, MRI scans, etc.).
  • Your insurance information, if applicable.
  • Comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows easy access to the area we will be treating (e.g., shorts for a knee problem, a tank top for a shoulder problem).

Save Time: Fill Out Forms Online

To make your first visit as smooth as possible, we encourage you to complete your new patient intake forms online through our secure patient portal. This saves you time in the waiting room and gives your therapist a chance to review your information beforehand.
